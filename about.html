Biography

About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Graduate Researcher in the Department of Human Intelligence Systems at Kyushu Institute of Technology (Kyutech), Japan, where I work in the Shibata Laboratory under Professor Takanori Shibata.

My research bridges machine learning, computer vision, and robotics — with a particular focus on building intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and interact with humans in real-world environments. I am deeply interested in how AI can enhance human-robot interaction and make assistive technology more accessible and effective.

Beyond research, I enjoy building practical AI tools and deploying machine learning models to production. I believe in open science and regularly share code, experiments, and insights through GitHub and this blog.
